# Welcome to My Profile! 🌟 🚀✨

He excels in Vulnerability Assessment, Penetration Testing, Senior SOC Analyst and Incident Response. His certifications include CASP+, CySA+, CEH, CND, and more. Daniel is well-versed in Splunk Engineering, SOAR, Threat hunting, and the MITRE ATT&CK framework. He's also adept at GRC frameworks like GDPR, ISO/IEC 27001, and NIST.
